Paneer Butter Masala

Delicious North Indian Curry

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Steps:

  1. Chop onions and tomatoes finely.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  5. Add paneer cubes and a little water. Cook for 5–7 minutes.
  6. Add cream and stir well.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Serving Suggestion:

Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and a small cube of butter.
